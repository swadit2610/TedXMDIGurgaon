The Golden Circle

Start with WHY - The core concept that changed everything

WHY
HOW
WHAT

WHY

Core belief, purpose, or cause - the reason you exist

Example: Apple believes in thinking differently and challenging the status quo

HOW

The process or values that bring your WHY to life

Example: Apple designs beautiful, simple, and user-friendly products

WHAT

The tangible products or services you offer as proof of your WHY

Example: Apple makes computers, phones, and tablets
